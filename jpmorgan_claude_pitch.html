Value Measurement Framework

How We Calculate JPMorgan's Business Value

Transparent, measurable methodology for determining Claude's contribution to productivity and revenue

Quantifiable Metrics

Time Savings (Primary)

  • Baseline Measurement: Average hours per advisor for research, analysis, and client prep
  • Claude Impact: Reduction in time for same quality output
  • Value Calculation: Time saved × average advisor hourly cost × number of users
  • Example: 5 hours/week saved × $150/hour × 500 advisors = $19.5M annually

Revenue Attribution

  • Client Capacity: Additional clients served due to efficiency gains
  • Deal Velocity: Faster transaction completion in CIB
  • Cross-selling: Better insights leading to additional product sales
  • Client Retention: Improved service quality reducing attrition

Measurement Methodology

Before/After Comparison

  • Baseline Period: 3-month pre-implementation measurement
  • Implementation: 6-month pilot with monthly tracking
  • Control Groups: Similar teams without Claude for comparison
  • Statistical Validation: Ensure results are statistically significant

Joint Measurement Committee

JPMorgan & Anthropic Partnership

  • Monthly review of productivity metrics
  • Agreed-upon calculation methodologies
  • Independent validation where appropriate
  • Transparent reporting and documentation

Conservative Approach

  • Only count directly attributable improvements
  • Exclude benefits that can't be clearly measured
  • Use lower bound of estimated ranges
  • Account for learning curve and adoption time
